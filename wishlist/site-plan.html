Overview

Purpose

This is a site to make creating wishlists that contain content from various sites easier as all the links are stored in one place. It is also convient as it will save the data between sessions so you dont have to worry about losing all the items you have put on your list.

Audience

It is designed for people who want to keep track of things they want to buy themselves. As the list cant be shared with others due to the data persisting on your device it is useful to track what a person wants to buy for themselves. It can also be used as a planning document, a place to keep organaized what all the gifts youll be buying for the holidays are and where they are as well. And because you enter the price with the item it makes keeping track of your budget easier as well.

Dynamic elements

Javascript is used to dyanmically add and remove items from the list, as well as to store them on your device. It will do so by having an array of items, and it will append/pop items from the list as they are entered or deleted. It will use local storage to keep track of the data you have saved to the list. there will be item objects with the name, price, and url for each item to make everything standardized.
